⚡ QUICK FIX FOR EMAIL ERROR

Open: backend/.env

Find these TWO lines and change them:

1. EMAIL_SECURE=true  →  EMAIL_SECURE=false
2. EMAIL_PASS= kddd eluw szik vaemsend  →  EMAIL_PASS=kdddleuwzikvaemsend

(Remove the spaces from the password!)

Then restart backend:
- Stop backend (Ctrl+C)
- Run: npm run dev

✅ Emails will work now!

Why?
- Port 587 needs secure=false (uses STARTTLS)
- Gmail App Password must have NO spaces
